WHAT IS CUERT?

A Team of Passion and dedication!

The 1st team from Cairo University to participate in the Shell Eco-Marathon competition that aims at driving greenly powered cars.

We are many times winners of the first and second places in the international on & off-track awards powered by Shell Eco-Marathon.
